About Me

My name is Steven Tu (涂宏成). I am currently a project manager at Sixnology Inc., a mobile application start-up based in Hsinchu, Taiwan.

As a project manager, I focus my energy on the following tasks:

  • How to put myself in the user's shoes to discover customer needs.
  • How to prototype rapidly to seek feedback and validate hypotheses.
  • How to over-communicate with clients, designers and engineers to ensure the whole team is in sync.
  • How to increase the frequency of collisions between team memebers to foster collaboration.
  • How to provide clear, concise and correct documentation for tracking and reference.
  • How to gain business insights from data analysis to continue improving upon existing products.

Moreover, I manage content and social media marketing for the company's top-ranked second screen application - Timely.tv, gaining valuable experience in the interplay between content creation, socail media marketing and user acquisition.

English fluency is one of my strong suits. I scored 110 (out of 120) in the TOEFL Internet-based Test, and scored a perfect 990 in the TOEIC exam. In addition to verbal and written communication, I am capable of giving presentations in English as well.

I had two years' experience in venture capital, first as an investment analyst at Enspire Capital, an early-stage venture capital firm from Singapore, and later as a junior manager at Chunghwa Investment, the investment arm of Chunghwa Telecom.

Whether it is from an investor's perspective or from a developer's point of view, I firmly believe that "the best way to predict the future is to invent it." I am looking forward to making my contributions to projects that would change people's lives and help shaping the future.
